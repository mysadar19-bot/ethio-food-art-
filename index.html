The Chef

Michael Yohannes

Born in Addis Ababa and trained across Many 5 stars Hotel in Addis Ababa and UAE Dubai Italian Restaurant kitchens, Chef Michael Yohannes bridges the soul of Ethiopian cuisine with the discipline of contemporary fine dining. His philosophy is simple — every plate should tell a story of origin, season, and intention.

As a certified nutrition coach, he designs menus that are as nourishing as they are beautiful — balancing macronutrients, ethically sourced ingredients, and bold flavor profiles tailored to each guest's lifestyle and dietary needs.
